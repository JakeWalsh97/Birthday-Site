Ainura's 24th Birthday

My Poem for Ainura

"Are you Brazilian?"
"No, ha ha..... Do I look Brazilian?"
"No, but in a place like this, I thought you might be"
"Well, I'm different to everyone else.... Can't you see?"
--------------------------------------------------
Despacito, Gasolina, Freed from Desire
I bought her Tequila, she did not want something lighter
The room is starting to spin, but my feet aren’t moving
My hand below her chin, on the clothes I’ll be removing
--------------------------------------------------
But, she’s different to everyone else…. Can’t you see?
It all seems so natural, nothing seems to be forced
She always dreamt of this…. Always dreamt to be free
She had a tough life, in her eyes I can see, she’s been through the worst
--------------------------------------------------
She opens up to me, she tells me what’s on her mind
I slowly open up too, but it takes a little more time
“I want to see the Cliffs of Moher, I want to go to Spain
I want to touch the Eiffel Tower, I want to jump out of a plane”
--------------------------------------------------
I promise her the world, although sometimes it can be scary
To love someone so much, does not always leave room for me
After 1 year has passed, we have travelled all around Ireland
Trains, hotels, buses…. We’ve touched each corner of the land
--------------------------------------------------
Ainur thinks she’s the devil, but she has a heart of gold
Her only weakness seems to be…. She’s really afraid of getting old
I have learned a little Russian, “Ya Tebya Lublu”
But I don’t think I’ll ever be fluent, if I’m being honest with you
--------------------------------------------------
We still talk about it sometimes, the night that we first met
“Are you Brazilian?”; were my first words to her and these words I will never regret
